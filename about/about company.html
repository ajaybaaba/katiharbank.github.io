About my company

The Bank of katihar (BOK) is an Indian public sector banking and financial services company.Bank of katihar is an Indian banking and financial services company headquartered in katihar, Bihar. Bank of katihar which started as a micro-finance company in 2015, received banking licence by Reserve Bank of India in 2016.Bank of katihar became the 21th largest bank (2019) in India . According to the Annual Report of the bank of 2016-17, the Bank has 2 branches and 4 ATMs .Bank of katihar has Mr. Sanjeev jaishwal as Chairman And Miss. Supriya mishra is the founder cum managing director and chief executive officer.
